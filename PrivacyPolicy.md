# Privacy Policy – Fribaaja

*Effective: 29.4.2026*

Fribaaja is a disc golf scoring app. This policy explains how the app handles data.

**What data is stored?**  
The app stores player names, course information (name, holes, par values) and round scores locally on your device. No data is sent anywhere — everything stays on your own device.

**Third parties**  
The app does not use analytics, ads, or send any data to external services.

**Backup**  
The app includes an optional backup feature that allows you to export or import your data as a JSON file. Sharing this file is entirely at the user's discretion.

**Deleting your data**  
You can delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device.
